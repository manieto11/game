A possible idea for this game could be about a robot that gains consciousness and decides to betray his kind and help humanity.
The settings of the game would be a post-apocalyptic city. Having some sci-fy elements like other robots (some of them would be enemies), some futuristic lights, maybe scrap parts (or robot parts). 
The player's robot could be able to scrap some parts from the boss battle bots and he should be able to implement them to itself (or maybe sell it for some currency).
The player's robot backstory could be that it stumbled upon a hidden message, or maybe a kid kidnapped a poor robot and hijacked his "brain".
